Introducing Columner Deux

Columner was a theme originally created by Mathieu Hardiviller. It was inspired by late 90's brutalist typographic web design, grids, horizontalism and of course, Indexhibit.

Columner Deux is a continuation of this theme but regenerated for Indexhibit and a more modern internet.

Check out how well it works with a variety of formats then download it and make it as you wish.
